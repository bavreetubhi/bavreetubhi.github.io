Most Recent Project

Composite Overwrapped Combustion Chamber

Solaris MkIII was the 3rd major iteration of the hybrid engine developed by Monash HPR. The combustion chamber was a type V COPV (Composite Overwrapped Pressure Vessel) designed to withstand 60 bar. A full suite of sims were completed to ensure structural integrity under operation. The chamber was tested at Race2Space using the Airborne test facility in the UK.

This project represents countless hours of design, simulation, manufacturing, and testing while learning new skills. Over 500 hours have gone into making the composite combustion chamber a reality. The image above shows the finished chamber ready for integration and testing.
