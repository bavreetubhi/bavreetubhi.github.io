Most Recent Project

Composite Overwrapped Combustion Chamber

Solaris MkIII was the 3rd Major itteration of the hybrid engine developed my Monash HPR, The Combustion chamber was a type V COPV (Composite Overwrapped Pressure Vessel) designed to withdstand 60 Bar, a full suite of sims were compleated in order to ensure structual intergrety under opperation. The chamber was teseted at Race2Space using the Airborne test facility in the UK.

This project represents countless hours of work designing, simulating, manufacturing and testing all while learning new skills. Over 500 hours have gone into making the composite combustion chamber a reality.
